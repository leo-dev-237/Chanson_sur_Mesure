Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to create a song?

The delivery time depends on the chosen plan: 48h for the "Simple Melody", 72h for the "Complete Creation", and 5 days for the "VIP Experience".

Can I request revisions?

Absolutely! Every order includes one free revision to ensure the song perfectly matches your expectations.

What musical styles do you offer?

We cover a wide range of styles: Pop, Rock, R&B, Makossa, Bikutsi, Afrobeat, romantic ballads, and many more upon request.

How does the payment work?

We accept Mobile Money (MTN, Orange), bank transfers, and cash payments in Yaoundé. A 50% down payment is required to start.

Ready to create an unforgettable memory?

Contact us directly on WhatsApp. Our team is ready to listen to your story and turn it into music.
